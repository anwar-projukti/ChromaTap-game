How to Play

  1. Watch the SequenceEach round begins with a sequence of colored tiles lighting up one by one. Pay close attention to the order — the sequence grows by one step with every new round.
  2. Tiles ShuffleAfter the sequence is shown, all tiles instantly shuffle to new positions. The colors are the same, but the spots have changed. Remember the COLOR, not where it was located.
  3. Tap in OrderTap the tiles in the exact order they glowed during the watch phase. Speed matters — faster correct taps earn bonus points. A wrong tap costs you a life.
  4. Beat Your BestSurvive as many rounds as possible. Chain correct answers to build streaks for multiplied points. Climb the local leaderboard and beat your high score.

Core Gameplay Features

Unique Shuffle Mechanic

Three distinct phases per round: Watch Phase, Shuffle Phase, and Tap Phase. Players must track color identity across randomised tile positions — a fresh spin on classic memory games.

Adaptive Difficulty

Three selectable difficulty modes (Easy, Normal, Hard) with dynamic grid sizing, increasing sequence lengths, and progressively shorter display timers as levels advance.

Streak Multipliers

Correct round chains build streak multipliers, rewarding consistent performance with bonus points and encouraging replay to climb the leaderboard.

Lives System

Players start each session with three lives. A wrong tap or timeout deducts a life. Losing all lives ends the session and displays a detailed results screen.

Hint System via Rewarded Ads

Players can watch a rewarded ad to unlock a hint that highlights the next correct tile. Clean integration with the AdMob Rewarded Ad API.

Round Timer

A countdown timer on the Tap Phase adds pressure and rewards speed bonuses, ensuring every session remains engaging and challenging.
